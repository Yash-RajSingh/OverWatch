Overwatch

We at OverWatch have a vision of making this world a better and safer place for each and every member of the society.

About

On OverWatch, you can have a look at the unsafe zones in your city on a map based on the experience of citizens around you, you can report any location as unsafe based on your experience , people who have witnessed any crime can share their experience with others so that others can learn from their experience and be aware and stay safe by avoiding these unsafe zones. You can read about the experience that led to people to report a location as a unsafe zone & post your own experience about any location in your city.

We believe if Overwatch is successful and people start sharing their experiences, mishappenings because of crimes can be averted from ever happening. If Overwatch recieves active participation, the goverment bodies like the police can use it to track the locations where crime rates are rising and increase security on those locations.

Instruction

After entering Overwatch, go to "Unsafe Zones" to know about unsafe places in your city.

  • To report a location as unsafe, visit :-"Unsafe Zones".
  • If you want to post your experience about any unsafe place or if you want to read other peoples experiences visit :-"Stories".
  • If you want to post your experience about an unsafe location but are worried for your safety and still want to help, just check "Post Anonymously". Overwatch does not use individual account feature so that the responsible citizens cannot be tracked in any way possible.

Stay safe, Rest assured, because we are watching over you!
